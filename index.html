Children's Privacy Notice: This app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

1. Introduction

Scottish Ski Resorts ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Scottish Ski Resorts mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't collect:

  • We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT require user accounts or registration
  • We do NOT collect photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • We do NOT track browsing history across other apps or websites

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

Our App automatically collects minimal technical information necessary for the App to function:

  • Anonymous Usage Data: Basic app usage statistics (e.g., which screens are viewed) to improve the App
  • Crash Reports: Technical information when the App crashes to help us fix bugs
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system version to ensure compatibility

This information is anonymous and cannot be used to identify individual users.

3. How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide ski resort information and weather conditions
  • Maintain and improve App functionality
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Analyze App performance and usage patterns

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Data storage, crash reporting, and analytics
  • Data Collected: Anonymous usage statistics and crash reports
  • Privacy Policy: Firebase Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do NOT knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • The App does NOT require personal information to function
  • We do NOT use persistent identifiers to track children
  • We do NOT enable children to publicly post or distribute personal information
  • There are NO in-app purchases or advertisements
  • There are NO chat features or user-generated content

Parental Notice

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately at the email below, and we will delete such information from our systems.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Data is stored securely using Firebase's encrypted servers
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access
  • Data is retained only as long as necessary for App functionality
  • Offline cached data is stored locally on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the App

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Use the App without providing any personal information
  • Clear cached data by uninstalling and reinstalling the App
  • Contact us with questions about privacy practices

8. Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • Sell or rent user data to third parties
  • Share personal information with advertisers
  • Use data for marketing purposes

Anonymous analytics data may be shared with Firebase/Google solely for App improvement purposes.

9. International Users

The App is primarily designed for users in the United Kingdom. By using the App, you consent to the transfer and processing of data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • For significant changes, displaying a notification in the App

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Since we do not collect personal information, these rights do not apply. However, if you have questions, please contact us.
